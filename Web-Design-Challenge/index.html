Summary: Latitude vs. X

For this project, we researched how different the weather was throughout the world based on the equator. We needed to pull data from OpenWeatherMap API. We used data from over 500 different countries. After sorting through the APIs, we used Matplotlib to plot the data. Since our focus was on the distance from the equator, we used latitude to get our information. This site will provide the plots that were created.
